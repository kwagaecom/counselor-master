Frequently Asked Questions

Our Trauma and PTSD counselling is designed to help you process and heal from past experiences in a safe and supportive environment. We work with you to develop coping strategies, regain control, and rebuild your sense of safety and well-being.

Cancer counselling provides emotional and psychological support tailored to your specific needs during your cancer journey. Whether you're dealing with the initial diagnosis, treatment challenges, or survivorship, our counselling helps you navigate each stage with strength and clarity.

Our Cardiovascular Diseases counselling focuses on supporting your heart health by helping you manage stress, lifestyle changes, and the emotional impact of living with a heart condition. We aim to enhance your quality of life through personalized care and advice.

Stress Management counselling offers techniques and strategies to help you manage stress more effectively. By addressing the root causes of your stress and providing tools to cope, our sessions can lead to a more balanced and peaceful life.

Chronic Pain Conditions counselling is tailored to help you manage the physical and emotional challenges of living with chronic pain. We provide coping mechanisms, lifestyle adjustments, and emotional support to improve your overall well-being.
